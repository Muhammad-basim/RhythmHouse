About us

RhythmHouse

'Rhythm House' is a music store in the city which offers not only music but contests and live shows too. It caters to almost all the age groups by providing the CDs, tapes of poems for kids, latest pop music collection for teenagers etc. Rhythm House also caters to a slightly older age group and boasts of the largest collection of regional and devotional music. Besides, if someone wants to pick up a good 60's or 70's rock album, chances are that he will get it here.
